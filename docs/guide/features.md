# Features

This section provides an overview of the key features available on the TipKoro platform.

## Creator Profiles

Every creator on TipKoro has a unique, customizable profile page. This page includes:

- **Username and Display Name:** Your unique identifier and display name.
- **Profile Picture:** A custom avatar to represent you or your brand.
- **Bio:** A short description to tell your audience about yourself.
- **Social Links:** Links to your other social media profiles.

Fans can visit your profile page to send you tips and see your latest activity.

## Tipping

Tipping is at the heart of TipKoro. We make it easy for fans to support their favorite creators.

- **Tipping Form:** On each creator's profile, there is a simple form to send a tip.
- **Payment Methods:** Users can choose from a variety of payment methods, all securely processed by Rupantor Pay.
- **Personalized Messages:** Fans can leave a message with their tip.

## Dashboard

The creator dashboard is your central hub for managing your TipKoro account. From the dashboard, you can:

- **View Your Stats:** See your total earnings, number of tips, and other useful analytics.
- **Manage Your Profile:** Update your profile information, including your bio and social links.
- **See Your Tip History:** A detailed log of all the tips you have received.

## Live Tips Feed

The homepage features a live feed of the latest tips on the platform. This is a great way to discover new creators and see the community's activity.
