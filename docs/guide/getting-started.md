# Getting Started

Welcome to TipKoro! This guide will walk you through the initial steps of creating an account and setting up your creator profile.

## Authentication

TipKoro uses Clerk for secure and easy authentication. You can sign up or log in using your existing social accounts (like Google or GitHub) or with a traditional email and password.

- **Sign Up:** Click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage to create a new account.
- **Log In:** If you already have an account, click "Log In" to access your dashboard.

## Becoming a Creator

Becoming a creator on TipKoro is a simple two-step process. This ensures that we have verified creators on our platform.

### Step 1: Initial Signup and Payment

1.  After creating your account, navigate to the "Become a Creator" page.
2.  You will be prompted to make a one-time payment through our secure payment partner, Rupantor Pay. This is a verification fee.
3.  You will be redirected to Rupantor Pay to complete the payment.

### Step 2: Complete Your Profile

1.  After a successful payment, you will be redirected back to TipKoro.
2.  You will now be able to fill out your creator profile information, including your username, display name, and other details.
3.  Once you submit your profile information, your creator profile will be live and you can start receiving tips!
